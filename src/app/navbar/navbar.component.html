Guess The Number

Read The Instructions Carefully
  • Enter Your Name & Click on The Submit Button.
  • Let's Start the Game.
  • Computer will guess a number(between 1 to 100).
  • On each attempt, you have to guess that number.
  • High: The number you guessed is higher than the number chosen by the computer.
    Low: The number you guessed is lower than the number chosen by the computer.
  • If you guessed it right, Tadaa!! You Win the game or else keep Trying.
